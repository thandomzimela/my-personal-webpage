About Me

My name is Thando Mzimela-Ntuli, and I am a student at the University of Cape Town studying BsocScie in Information Systems. I have a passion for systems analysis, business analysis, and startups. As the founder and CEO of uniMark, I am dedicated to creating a digital platform that centralizes services for university students, making it easier for them to find and book these services.

Beyond my startup ventures, I am an active student leader. I sit on the Dean's Advisory Board for Dean Professor Shose Kessi at the University of Cape Town, serve on the Humanities Faculty Board, and am the Chairman of the Students Council and a member of the Student Parliament. Additionally, I am a member of the Undergraduate Academic Sub-Council, a sub-council of the SRC. I have a deep passion for technology, particularly in user experience and interface, product development, and design.
